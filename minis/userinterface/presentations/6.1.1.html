Notifications.


Activity Alerts.


Notifications that alert that user about activity that is occurring within the system that they are not yet aware of.

Enticement.


Notifications designed to entice the user to return to the application in order to further engage with the system.

FOMO.


Notifications are often designed to heighten the user's sensation of the fear of missing out when they are not actively engaged with the system.

Gamification.


Incentives.


Incentives are a mechanism for rewarding a level of engagement from the user.

Achievements.


Achievements are a simulated reward for participatory activities that the user has been involved in.

Competition.


Competition is a context in which people's individual performance can be placed in order to challenge them to engage with the system at a higher level.
